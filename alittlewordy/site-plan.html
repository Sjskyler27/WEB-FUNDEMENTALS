A Little Wordy Site Plan

Skyler Simpson

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the A Little Wordy website is to provide a fun and engaging online platform for users to play a word game similar to Scrabble. The website aims to offer users an easy-to-use interface, clear instructions, and an enjoyable user experience.

Audience

The target audience for A Little Wordy includes people of all ages who enjoy word games and puzzles. This audience includes casual gamers, word enthusiasts, and couples who want to spend quality time together. The website will cater to users who are comfortable with computers and mobile devices.
